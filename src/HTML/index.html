    • Places to eat

    • Erimitis

      Erimitis is a traditional taverna located in the village of Gaios. It offers a range of Greek dishes made with fresh, locally-sourced ingredients. The restaurant has a cozy atmosphere and is a popular spot for both locals and tourists.

    • Taverna Vasilis

      Taverna Vasilis is a family-run restaurant located in the village of Loggos. It specializes in traditional Greek cuisine and offers a variety of meat and seafood dishes. The restaurant has a beautiful view of the sea and is known for its friendly service.

    • Café Bar Levrechio

      Café Bar Levrechio is a modern café and bar located in the center of Gaios. It offers a range of coffee, cocktails, and snacks, as well as light meals and desserts. The café has a cozy and relaxed atmosphere and is a popular spot for both locals and tourists.

    • Taverna Arriva

      Taverna Arriva is a traditional taverna located in the village of Longos. It offers a range of Greek dishes made with fresh, locally-sourced ingredients. The restaurant has a cozy atmosphere and is a popular spot for both locals and tourists.

    • O Nikolas

      O Nikolas is a family-run restaurant located in the village of Loggos. It specializes in traditional Greek cuisine and offers a variety of meat and seafood dishes. The restaurant has a cozy atmosphere and is known for its friendly service.

    Places to see

    • Gaios Village

      The capital and main port of Paxos, Gaios is a charming village with colorful houses, narrow streets, and a picturesque waterfront.

    • Lakka Village

      A quaint fishing village on the north coast of Paxos, Lakka is known for its beautiful beaches, waterfront tavernas, and vibrant atmosphere.

    • Ozias Beach

      A secluded and stunning beach on the east coast of Paxos, Ozias is surrounded by lush greenery and features crystal-clear waters and powdery sand.

    • Antipaxos Island

      A small island located just south of Paxos, Antipaxos is famous for its breathtaking beaches, including Voutoumi and Vrika, and its vibrant turquoise waters.

    • Erimitis Beach

      A scenic beach on the west coast of Paxos, Erimitis is surrounded by towering cliffs and features a picturesque chapel and stunning sunsets.
